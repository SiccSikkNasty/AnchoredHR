Overview

How It Works

Three-Step Engagement Process

1.     Discovery Call (30 min): Brief HR and ER risk scan; understand your priorities, current gaps, and growth plans

2.     Proposal & Pilot Plan (2 weeks): Custom 90-day or project roadmap with clear outcomes, deliverables, and entry point

3.     Ongoing Partnership: Retainer or project delivery with regular check-ins, proactive support, and documented progress

Low-friction entry: Pilot with a 3-month retainer or single fixed project; extend or scale as value becomes clear.

Mission

Outcome Focus

Reduce risk, build confident supervisors, and prepare organizations for situations that may escalate to legal counsel or external processes.

Typical Inclusions

·       Employment-standards and HR compliance scans with practical, prioritized recommendations

·       Employee relations case support: discipline and performance planning, investigation guidance, documentation coaching, preparation for counsel meetings

·       For union or union-vulnerable workplaces: communication strategies, grievance-handling processes, supervisor training on documentation and respectful labour-relations practices

·       Clear integration with employment counsel when required (always non-legal advice with transparent boundaries)

Why

Why choose AnchoredHR?

For owners & leaders:

When you need a trusted HR colleague, someone who knows how approach the more legal workforce areas as applied to Human Resource Management, while understanding real-world complexities, without high cost, or inflated rates and fees. AnchoredHR is that partner that is calm, steady, knowledgeable, and invested in your team's stability and your organization's potential.

For organizations:

Compliance, confidence, and capability without the full-time cost. Whether you're navigating growth, managing conflict, building systems, policies and procedures, or preparing for change, AnchoredHR anchors you through every stage.

Our approach is based on experience, having spent many years in day-to-day situational environments, whether in private, public, unionized, or non-union, the approach is the same, we work together as colleagues, finding solutions to problems with an evidence-based approach.
